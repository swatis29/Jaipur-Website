JAIPUR

Jaipur, is the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Rajasthan. Jaipur is also known as the Pink City,
due to the dominant colour scheme of its buildings. It is also known as the Paris of India.

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal is in Jaipur, India. Built from red and pink sandstone.

Jal Mahal

Jal Mahal is a palace in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake in Jaipur city.

Sheesh Mahal

Sheesh mahal also called as mirror palace it is the most beautiful part of Amer Fort.

Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir, Jaipur (Lakshmi Narayan Temple) is a Hindu temple located in Jaipur, India.
